About Our Legacy

Navrathan Jewellers and Bankers, established in 2007 by the visionary Madanlal Parihar and Mangalaram Parihar, stands as a beacon of trust and authenticity in Bangalore's jewellery and financial landscape.

Our collection is especially loved for its authentic gold and silver designs, thoughtfully curated for the Muslim community — with a variety of options available for others as well. What truly sets us apart is our dual expertise: we also operate as licensed bankers, providing secure and transparent pledging facilities, making us your unique destination for both precious ornaments and reliable financial services.

Transparent Gold & Silver Pledging

At Navrathan Jewellers and Bankers, we pride ourselves on offering exceptionally safe, transparent, and digitally managed gold and silver pledging services. Our valid banking license ensures complete trust and efficiency in every transaction, making your financial needs our priority.

  • Competitive Financing Rates: Benefit from highly competitive and customer-friendly financing rates designed to make borrowing accessible and easy.
  • Streamlined Digital Process: Our pledging process is entirely managed through a secure digital platform, significantly reducing paperwork, waiting times, and unnecessary delays.
  • Swift & Hassle-Free Service: Enjoy a fast and efficient experience, with instant evaluations and quick approvals, ensuring you get the funds you need without delay.
  • Valid ID Proof Required: For your security and compliance, a valid government-issued photo identification is required to complete the pledging process.
  • Flexible Pledge Duration: Your valuable ornaments are securely stored for up to 1 year, with a generous grace period of 15 days for your convenience.
  • Easy Renewable Terms: Pledges can be easily renewed, offering continued financial flexibility and peace of mind without needing to redeem and re-pledge.
