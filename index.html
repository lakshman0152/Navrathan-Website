About Our Shop

ShinySpark Jewellery is a family-run boutique jewellery store based in Mumbai, India. Established in 1995, we specialize in creating timeless, handcrafted jewellery with purity, precision, and passion.

Our craftsmanship blends traditional artistry with modern elegance, making every piece a symbol of grace and authenticity.

Meet the Owners

Founded by Mr. Rajesh Sharma and Mrs. Nidhi Sharma, ShinySpark began as a small workshop and grew into a trusted name in the jewellery world.

Their son, Arjun Sharma, now leads the business into the digital age with the same values of trust, quality, and design excellence.
