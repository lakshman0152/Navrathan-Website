About Our Shop

Navrathan Jewellers and Bankers was established in 2007 by Madanlal and Mangalaram. Our store is trusted for genuine gold and silver jewellery in Bangalore, especially known for styles preferred by the Muslim community.

We also provide pledging facilities as we hold a valid banking license, making us a unique combination of jeweller and banker.

Management

The shop is proudly managed by Mangalaram, continuing the family tradition of trust and excellence in service.

What We Sell

  • Silver Ornaments – All categories and styles
  • Pledging Services – Available under our licensed banking facility
